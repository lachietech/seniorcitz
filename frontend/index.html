Welcome to the Sandgate & District Senior Citizens Centre

A vibrant, welcoming space for our local seniors to connect, thrive, and enjoy life in Sandgate.

About Us

Established in the heart of Sandgate, our Senior Citizens Centre has been a place of friendship, fun, and community since 1952. We provide a supportive and inclusive space where older adults can stay active, social, and engaged through a variety of programs and services.
